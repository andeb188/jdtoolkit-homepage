The City of Brother Love

This booklet explores the issues of the ongoing epidemic in Philadelphia: gun violence. It has effected nearly every single resident of Philadelphia, apparently, except our own leaders who plan meetings, but take little action. This is about the victims, the history of Philadelphia and how did we get here, and even personal reflections. After writing this, I am even more angrier than before about it.
